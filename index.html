denoise-voice-clarity is a free, license-free noise-suppression filter for WebRTC calls — a drop-in Krisp replacement built on DeepFilterNet 3, running 100% client-side via WebAssembly. Try it below with your own microphone.

🔒 No audio leaves your device ⚡ ~Real-time, on the audio thread 🧩 LiveKit + plain WebRTC
